Bungalow in the Broads

Riverside Rentals has a wonderful new addition to our range of holiday properties! Located in the beautiful broads haven, Wroxham, this thatched bungalow offers stunning views of the famous waterway. Wake up to the gentle sounds of nature, metres from the water. Enjoy local attractions such as Bewilderwood, or hire a day boat and float leisurely along the broads. The bungalow is maintained to an exacting standard, and, as with all our properties, cleaning and fresh linen are provided at no extra cost for the duration of your stay. Information on things to do in the area is also available on request.
